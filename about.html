About Tamani Tea

Bringing the authentic taste of premium Kenyan tea to homes, cafés, hotels, and tea lovers around the world.

Who We Are

Tamani Tea Ltd was incorporated in 2024 as a trading entity exporting and importing commodities and products.
The company therefore intends to venture into bulk tea export and later tea value addition to comply with the tea Act 2021.

We believe tea is more than just a drink — it is an experience of comfort, freshness, culture, and connection. Our carefully selected tea leaves are processed with passion and dedication to ensure every cup is refreshing and memorable.

Our mission is to provide the world with the finest, most ethically sourced tea,fostering a sustainable and equitable tea industry while delighting our customers with exceptional flavor and quality.
